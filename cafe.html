Breakfast

Start your day with hearty farm-fresh fare at our café, featuring fluffy buttermilk pancakes drizzled with locally sourced honey, crispy bacon from our own pigs, and freshly squeezed orange juice straight from the grove. Indulge in our homemade granola served with creamy yogurt and seasonal berries, or savor our farm-fresh omelette crafted with free-range eggs and garden vegetables, accompanied by a piping hot cup of fair-trade coffee.

Lunch

Savor the flavors of the countryside with our farm-to-table lunch menu, featuring a variety of wholesome options. Enjoy our signature farmhouse salad bursting with crisp greens, ripe tomatoes, and crunchy cucumbers harvested from our fields, accompanied by a selection of artisanal cheeses and freshly baked bread. Delight in our savory quiche made with eggs from our hens and seasonal vegetables, or indulge in a classic farm burger crafted from grass-fed beef.

Afternoon Tea

Indulge in a quintessentially charming afternoon tea experience amidst the serene countryside setting of our farm café. Treat yourself to a selection of delicate finger sandwiches filled with locally sourced ingredients, complemented by freshly baked scones served with clotted cream and homemade preserves. Sip on aromatic loose-leaf teas brewed to perfection, or opt for a bubbly glass of prosecco to elevate your afternoon delight.
