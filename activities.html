Petting Zoo

The farm's petting zoo offers visitors the chance to interact closely with a variety of friendly animals, fostering a hands-on learning experience in a picturesque rural setting. Guests can engage with everything from gentle goats and soft rabbits to chirping chicks, creating memorable moments for all ages.

Play Park

Nestled within the farm's grounds, the play park provides a lively haven for children, featuring swings, slides, and climbing structures amidst the rustic charm of the countryside. Families enjoy endless fun and adventure as they explore the play park's attractions, creating cherished memories against the backdrop of rolling fields and grazing animals.

Outdoor Walks

Embarking on outdoor walks at the farm offers a tranquil escape into nature, where visitors can wander along winding paths amidst verdant fields and whispering trees. With each step, guests immerse themselves in the serene beauty of the countryside, encountering picturesque landscapes and perhaps glimpses of grazing livestock along the way.
